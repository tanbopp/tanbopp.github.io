About Me

A Software Engineer and Graphic Designer based in Jakarta, Indonesia, currently serving as Founder & AI Native Engineer at Centrova Teknologi Indonesia, where I build software solutions from the ground up using an AI-driven development approach.

My journey began in graphic design and automotive product photography at Otoproject Group, where I handled the creative process end-to-end — from professional vehicle photography, photo editing, and catalogue design, to packaging design and 3D product modeling. This experience sharpened my attention to detail, composition, and brand consistency in every piece of work I produce.

My interests later expanded into application development, particularly within the Flutter ecosystem for mobile and web development. I enjoy solving technical problems and delivering solutions that are not only functional, but also scalable and maintainable in the long run.

This combination of visual design background and software engineering makes me comfortable working across different roles — whether as a UI Designer focused on user experience, or as a FullStack Developer who brings that vision to life. I believe cross-functional collaboration is key to building products that truly meet both business goals and user needs.
